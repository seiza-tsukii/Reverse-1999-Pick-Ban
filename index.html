How does the pick & ban work for Reverse:1999?

Each player participates in the draft, where they will select their 8 characters. Once this is done, both players compose their 2 teams, then complete Limbo-5 and Limbo-6. The player who takes the fewest turns wins the game.

This format is inspired by the Genshin Impact pick & ban (which you can find here: https://genshinbanpick.top/) created by u/Frostwyrm9x (on Reddit). This site was also created with the help of Deepseek (I'm bad at JSON, sorry...), so feel free to customize it!
